Understanding the QIZ Advantage

What is QIZ?

The Qualified Industrial Zone agreement is a trade partnership between Egypt, the U.S., and Israel that eliminates customs duties entirely.

Requirements:

35% of materials/labor must come from QIZ-designated areas in Egypt

(We handle ALL compliance)

Your Benefits:

0% customs fees instead of 10% standard duty
